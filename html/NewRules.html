New Rules

The new premier league season kicked off and there has been a few rule changes that
not many fans have seemed to be aware of, so today we will look at them.


VAR



VAR was introduced last season and so there are many new rules and regulations
in VAR. These are to make the game go faster and to have less input from VAR.
VAR will now only be used on fouls or potential fouls within 30 yards from the goals
out of this range means that the foul must be clear and obvious. Offsides now must be
very clear and the player must be offside by more then 1 inch.


Throw Ins

Throw ins will now have a 10 second time limit and warnings will be issued and yellows
or reds issued if nead be.


Goal Posts

All football goal posts must now be 8 foot apart.
