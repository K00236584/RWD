Mbappe

After bursting onto the world news nearly three years ago now,
Mbappe has rocked the footballing world with his amazing blend of
pace and skill. He is seen as one of the main factors for Monaco's
success in winning the Ligue 1 and upsetting the league favourites
Paris-Saint Germain. Many former players haven't been shy to heap
praise onto the boy. The young 20 year old has been labeled as the
newest football potential breakthrough of the dominanent pairing of the
worlds best and potentially the best of all time, Lionel Messi and
Cristiano Ronaldo.

Currently Mbappe is showing the world why he is not a one season
wonder and has been tearing up the Ligue 1 again, this time for the
team that he toppled PSG. In his combined 12 matches in the Ligue 1 and
champions league, he has 9 goals, not an easy feat and much better then
Ronaldo or Messi was at this stage in their careear. Although, the one

thing that Messi and Ronaldo have over Mbappe is each other. Mbappe
doesn't really have a opposition driving force to push him to be the
best like Ronaldo and Messi did for each other. Mbappe also has a few
downsides to him. Many say that most players can put up these stats as,
PSG have a stigma for getting very easy group draws and going out once
they come up against a top team. The ligue 1 has also been known as a
one team league with PSG's riches and complete dominance.

Mbappe doesn't seem keen to leave his home country soon though and many
believe that he will never leave the French capital during his prime
footballing years.
