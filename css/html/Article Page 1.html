GOAT VS GOAT!

On July 25th Jonathan Rodgers left his 15 year home of Boston to go and play for an
oppostition team, now 3 months later he returns facing his former team and most
importantly his other half. Legendary coach Bill McCarthy who it is rumored to
have forced Rodgers out has been linked to Rodgers once they both came into the
league will now face Rodgers for the first time. McCarthy has had a great start
without his former star, winning 5 and drawing 1. Rodgers who has been unable to
win a game so far with 3 draws and 3 losses. For this reason McCarthy and Boston
are favoured by 3 points. While many say that on paper Boston should be favoured
by much more, the boys in Vegas have more confidence in Rodgers becoming more relaxed
within his new team. Last week he linked up with star running back James Leford to
rush for a combined 180 yards. This might not be very helpful to Rdogers as Boston
has become almost masters at defending the run, keeping last years best running back
to only 25 yards in as many carries. Rodgers has done the impossible before making
an amazing comeback from 18 points down while injured in just 5 minutes. Back then
he had McCarthy’s defense forcing a fumble and an interception. According to former
Boston wide reciver Julian Adams, he has more belief in his quarterback then the coach.
“We won mostly inspite of coach [McCarthy] and almost all of that was on Rodgers’
shoulders. Coach is a foul and he is even more of a foul if he believes that he will beat him.”
This is a massive strike at McCarthy who has had his credentials questioned in the past.
Many former coaches have stated that McCarthy is an offensive mastermind. Making the
modern 'ground and pound' rushing style, which has made many younger quarterback's
lives and early career's much easier. This has also put into question Rodgers'
own credentials and many see him to be a 'game manager', which his current record
does add to the argument.
